🧠 What is Bayes’ Theorem?

Bayes’ Theorem helps you update your beliefs when new information arrives.
Instead of sticking to old probabilities, you refine them based on the latest evidence.

Simple Meaning:

“How should my probability change after learning something new?”

From medical testing to weather forecasting to AI predictions — Bayes' theorem powers intelligent reasoning everywhere.
